Game rules :
  • Only one disc can be moved at a time.
  • A larger disc cannot be placed on top of a smaller disc.
  • Only the top disc from any tower may be moved to another tower.
  • Click on tower to select the top disc and drop it on another tower by clicking on it.
  • Click on same tower to unselect the disc.
  • Move all discs to last tower to finish the game.
Best played in desktop full screen mode.
